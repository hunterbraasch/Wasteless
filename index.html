We all hate food waste for many reasons but hey, — life happens. Food goes bad sooner than we expect, we forget the leftovers and let’s not even mention that bag of lettuce always lost in the crisper drawer.

That’s why the Wasteless containers and app are an essential pair: they help you keep your fridge at your fingertips, know the freshness status of your food and get recipes and ideas customized to your ingredients.

Waste less for a better planet

Our idea started from identifing a problem: too much of the food produced ends up in the landfill, contributing to our carbon footprint and taking a huge toll on the planet. At the same time, 1 in 8 Americans struggle to put food on the table on a daily basis.

Our core value is to do better for you, our customers, and for the planet. Aside from using glass containers with tops made out of recycled plastic or bamboo, proceeds from your purchase also support great initiatives as we launch.
